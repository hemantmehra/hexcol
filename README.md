# hexcol
A columnar database

## Design
1. Storing tables by column rather than by rows. 

Example:
```
 ID  |  Name  |  Email              | Age |
  1  | user1  | person1@example.com | 25  |
  2  | user2  | person2@example.com | 26  |
  3  | user3  | person3@example.com | 31  |
  .      .               .            .
  .      .               .            .
  .      .               .            .
```
